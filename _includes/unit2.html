You will need to support floating point numbers your calculations. Try to implement your solution using list comprehensions.
    • Write a function to compute the factoral of a number. Formally, factorial of N (written N!) is defined as: 
      N * (N - 1) * (N - 2) * ... 1
      Implement two versions of your solution, one iterative and one recursive.
